I am an AI Researcher at [**KRAFTON**](https://www.krafton.com/). Before joining KRAFTON, I received my M.S. from the [**Graduate School of AI, KAIST**](https://gsai.kaist.ac.kr/), advised by [**Prof. Jinwoo Shin**](https://alinlab.kaist.ac.kr/shin.html).

I aspire to build **multimodal AI agents** that can see, listen, speak, and act as naturally as humans, enabling truly seamless human-AI interaction. Toward this goal, my research spans:
- Multimodal perception and reasoning across vision, language, and speech
- Autonomous agent learning and evaluation in interactive environments
- Generative models for vision and speech

## News

- 🔊 Our team at KRAFTON released the [**Raon**](https://huggingface.co/KRAFTON) model family, a suite of four multimodal models for speech understanding and generation. I was deeply involved in building [**Raon-Speech-9B**](https://huggingface.co/KRAFTON/Raon-Speech-9B) and [**Raon-SpeechChat-9B**](https://huggingface.co/KRAFTON/Raon-SpeechChat-9B).
- 📝 1 paper accepted to **CVPR 2026** (See and Fix the Flaws).
- 📝 2 papers accepted to **ICLR 2026** (Orak, VLM-SubtleBench). See you in Rio! 🇧🇷
